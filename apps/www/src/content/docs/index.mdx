Your docs are the first thing anyone — human or agent — meets before your
product. Nimbus is how you make them count: docs you own, built on Astro,
agent-native from day one.

```sh
npx @cloudflare/create-nimbus-docs@latest
```

## Why Nimbus

- **You own every file.** No upstream theme to fork around — and agents reason better when nothing's hidden.
- **Humans and agents, first-class.** Reading and authoring for both, out of the box.
- **Slop stays out.** A prose linter keeps humans and agents honest; drafts ship tagged, not trusted.
