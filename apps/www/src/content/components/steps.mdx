## Examples

### Basic

Three Steps with text bodies — counting and connecting lines are automatic.

<Example code={`<Steps>
  <Step>Clone the repository.</Step>
  <Step>Install dependencies with \`pnpm install\`.</Step>
  <Step>Run the dev server with \`pnpm dev\`.</Step>
</Steps>`} contentClass="w-full">
  <div class="w-full">
    <Steps>
      <Step>Clone the repository.</Step>
      <Step>Install dependencies with <code>pnpm install</code>.</Step>
      <Step>Run the dev server with <code>pnpm dev</code>.</Step>
    </Steps>
  </div>
</Example>

### With titles

Add a `title` prop for bold lead text, then body content below.

<Example code={`<Steps>
  <Step title="Install">
    Add the package to your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Edit \`config.json\` to match your environment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run">
    Start the dev server and open localhost.
  </Step>
</Steps>`} contentClass="w-full">
  <div class="w-full">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install">Add the package to your project.</Step>
      <Step title="Configure">Edit <code>config.json</code> to match your environment.</Step>
      <Step title="Run">Start the dev server and open localhost.</Step>
    </Steps>
  </div>
</Example>

### Custom start

Use `start` to continue numbering across a split flow.

<Example code={`<Steps start={5}>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Push to main; CI builds and deploys.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Open the deployed URL and confirm the staging banner.
  </Step>
</Steps>`} contentClass="w-full">
  <div class="w-full">
    <Steps start={5}>
      <Step title="Deploy">Push to main; CI builds and deploys.</Step>
      <Step title="Verify">Open the deployed URL and confirm the staging banner.</Step>
    </Steps>
  </div>
</Example>
