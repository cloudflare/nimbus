## Examples

### Aspect ratio

Lock the frame to a fixed ratio. Common choices: 16/9 (video), 4/3 (classic), 1/1 (square), 21/9 (ultrawide).

<Example code={`<Frame aspect="16/9" caption="16 / 9">

</Frame>

<Frame aspect="1/1" caption="1 / 1">

</Frame>

<Frame aspect="21/9" caption="21 / 9">
